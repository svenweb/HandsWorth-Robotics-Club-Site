Robots


Our current Robotics project is

    The Hydraulic Bot!

    Items you will need:

  • 1. Popsicle sticks(4.5") x50
  • 2. Craft cubes(0.5" x 0.5" x 0.5") x10
  • 3. Craft cubes with holes (0.5” x 0.5” x 0.5”; hole of 5/32” ) x10
  • 4. 1/8" dowels atleast 5-10
  • 5. 10mL plastic syringes x4
  • 6. Vinyl tubing (inside diameter of 1/8", outside diameter of 1/4”) 2-3 feet
  • 7. 4" cable ties at least 5-10
  • 8. Hot glue gun + Hot glue sticks ----------
  • 9. Food dye (optional) ----------

Where to buy:

1-4, 8-9: Michael’s, Walmart 5: Try asking at the pharmacy (London Drugs, Shoppers Drug Mart) for non-needle plastic syringes that are for cough syrup - helpful to mention that this is for a hydraulic bot project! 6-7: Home Depot
